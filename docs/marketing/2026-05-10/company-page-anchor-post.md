## Post copy

**Why we built Biotica**

The tools to track training, sleep, and body comp seriously already exist. They just don't talk to each other.

Oura tells you readiness. Withings tells you weight. Your training app tracks volume. Your phone's health app aggregates steps and heart rate. None of them know what the others know.

Biotica reads them as one signal and puts an AI coach on top.

What's in V1:
• Adaptive workout programming that responds to your readiness
• AI coach that answers training questions grounded in your actual data
• Lean body mass trends, not just weight noise
• Supplement tracking with prescription-interaction warnings
• Weekly performance brief

Solo build. Android first. iOS to follow once Apple Developer enrollment clears.

The backlog, the architectural decisions, and the bugs are visible in public. The goal is a product that ships and keeps shipping.

If you already track your body seriously and you're tired of holding the correlations in your head, follow along.
